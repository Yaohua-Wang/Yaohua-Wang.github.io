I am an Associate professor at School of Computer Science, National University of Defense Technology. I am also an affiliated senior researcher of SAFARI Research Group (D-INFK, ETH Zurich), Led by Prof. Dr. Onur Mutlu.

I received my PhD, MSc and BSc in Computer Engineering fomr Nation University of Defense Technology. My advisor is Prof. Shuming Chen, who leads the High Performance Microprocessor Innovation Group in Chinese Ministry of Education.  

My research centered on the general field of Computer Architecture, with a current focus on Memory Systems. I am also very interested in Domain-specific Designs for IoT devices and Embedded Systems, based on RISC-V.

We constantly works with well-known researchers from internationally-recognized institutions (e.g. ETH and CMU). If you are interested in Computer Architecture and ready to start your journey, don't hesitate to get in touch with me.
